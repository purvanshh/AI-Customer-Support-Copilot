# SupportCopilot — Knowledge Base

## Getting Started

### How do I create an account?
Visit example.com/signup and enter your email and a password. You will receive a verification email — click the link to activate your account.

### What browsers are supported?
We support the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. For the best experience use Chrome.

## Account & Security

### How do I reset my password?
Go to Settings > Security > Reset Password. You will receive a password-reset email. Click the link within 15 minutes to set a new password.

### How do I enable two-factor authentication (2FA)?
Navigate to Settings > Security > Two-Factor Authentication and follow the prompts. You can use an authenticator app (recommended) or SMS.

### Can I change my account email?
Yes. Go to Settings > Profile > Email and enter your new email. You will need to verify the new address before the change takes effect.

## Billing & Subscriptions

### What plans are available?
- **Free**: Up to 3 projects, 1 user
- **Basic** ($9/mo): Up to 10 projects, 5 users
- **Pro** ($29/mo): Unlimited projects, 20 users, priority support
- **Team** ($12/user/mo): 5+ seats, admin dashboard, SSO

### How do I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Go to Account > Subscription > Change Plan. Upgrades are prorated immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

### How do I cancel my subscription?
Go to Account > Subscription > Cancel. You will retain access until the end of your current billing period. Refunds are prorated.

### I was charged twice — what do I do?
Please contact support with your receipt or transaction ID. We will investigate and process a refund within 3-5 business days.

## Data & Import/Export

### What file formats do you support for import?
We support CSV, JSON, and XLSX for data imports. For programmatic access, use our REST API.

### How do I export my data?
Go to your project dashboard > Export. You can choose CSV or JSON format. Large exports are processed asynchronously and you will receive a download link via email.

### I accidentally deleted a project — can it be recovered?
Deleted projects are kept in a soft-delete state for 30 days. Go to Settings > Trash and click Restore. After 30 days, recovery is not possible.

## Troubleshooting

### The app crashes when exporting reports
Please update to the latest version (3.2.1+). If the problem persists, clear your browser cache and retry. If it still fails, contact support with the date range and browser version.

### Login keeps timing out
Clear your browser cookies for our domain and try again. If the issue persists, try an incognito window or a different browser. This is usually caused by an expired session token.

### API requests are returning 429 errors
You have exceeded the rate limit. Free-tier accounts are limited to 100 requests/minute. Upgrade to Pro for 1,000 requests/minute or contact us for custom limits.
